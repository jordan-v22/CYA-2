# Reflection – CYA-2

## Activity Description
The objective of this activity was to engage in a career-aligned experience and produce a verifiable deliverable. I chose to apply for a remote Computer Data Analyst position. As part of this process, I submitted application materials and progressed to receiving a request to schedule an interview.

## Technical and Professional Decisions
I chose to apply for a data analyst role because it aligns with my broader interest in cybersecurity and data-driven analysis. Many cybersecurity roles, especially in threat intelligence and forensics, rely heavily on analyzing structured and unstructured data. This role represents a practical entry point into that type of work.

## Contributions
This was an individual effort. I independently:
- Identified a relevant job opportunity
- Submitted an application
- Engaged with the employer through the interview scheduling process

## Quality Assessment
I believe this was a successful first step in professional development. Advancing to the interview stage indicates that my resume and application materials are competitive.

If I were to repeat this experience, I would:
- Apply to a larger number of roles to increase opportunities
- Track applications more systematically
- Prepare more extensively for technical interview questions

## Career Alignment
This experience directly supports my career goals by:
- Providing exposure to real hiring processes
- Reinforcing my interest in data-focused technical roles
- Building momentum toward internships or entry-level positions

Overall, this activity represents a concrete step toward transitioning from academic work into a professional technical role.
